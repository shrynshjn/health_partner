Privacy Policy

Your data. Your control. Your health journey.

Last Updated: January 2025

Health Partner is a personalized wellness assistant designed to help users track nutrition, workouts, sleep, hydration, and personal body metrics. We take privacy seriously — your health data is deeply personal, and protecting it is our priority.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Name, email, date of birth, and authentication credentials.
  • Health and Activity Logs: Food intake, workouts, sleep, water, physical parameters, and health report values.
  • Uploaded Media: Images associated with logs (e.g., food photos, workout screenshots, medical reports).
  • Technical Data: Device type, IP address, browser metadata (for security + debugging).

2. Purpose of Collecting Data

  • To generate personalized health insights and progress tracking.
  • To provide daily summaries, recommendations, and habit analytics.
  • To allow syncing and exporting of your health logs.
  • To maintain account security and prevent misuse.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored in encrypted cloud databases.
  • Authentication tokens and API keys are securely hashed.
  • Media files are stored in private secure storage (e.g., AWS S3).
  • Internal access to data is strictly limited to authorized systems.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.

We only share data in these cases:

  • You explicitly enable an integration (e.g., wearable sync).
  • Required by law (e.g., court order, legal compliance).

5. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account remains active. You may request deletion at any time.

6. Data Deletion & Export

You may request a full account deletion or export of your data by contacting:

privacy@healthpartner.shrynshjn.com

7. Your Rights

  • Access the data you have stored.
  • Request correction of inaccurate entries.
  • Request data deletion at any time.

8. Children’s Privacy

Health Partner is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Updates may occur — continued use means acceptance of future revisions.
